Animated brand identity for a creative studio. The brief was to make something that felt handmade even in motion, not slick corporate animation, but something with the texture of craft behind it.

Built in After Effects and delivered as a living system: logo animations, transitional elements, lower thirds, and a set of looping backgrounds that the studio could deploy across presentations and social.
