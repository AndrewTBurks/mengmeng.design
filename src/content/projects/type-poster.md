Experimental typographic posters combining letterpress and digital. The letterpress blocks were carved and printed in-studio, then overprinted with halftone textures and hand-inked distortions using digital processes.

The tension between the two processes, the press's physical imprint and the screen's reproducible precision, became the subject of the work itself.
