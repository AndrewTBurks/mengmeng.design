A collection of hand-drawn illustrations for a children's book. The project demanded a visual language that could be simultaneously naive and precise, loose enough to feel childlike, controlled enough to carry a narrative across sixty pages.

Pencil, ink, and watercolor, scanned and cleaned in Illustrator. Every character has three expressions and at least two costume variations.
